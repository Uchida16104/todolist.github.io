Tasks

    ▼How to use

    1.Enter your tasks in the input box above "Tasks" and click on the "Register" button to play the background music.


    At the same time, the text you have entered will appear under "Tasks".


    *Refreshing the page or deleting the tab will automatically delete the data you have entered, so we recommend that you save your data as a screenshot or pdf output beforehand.


    2.You can delete a task by clicking on the "Delete" button next to the text of the task list that appears.


    3.You can change the background color by clicking on the "Change background color" button.

    (Ex1) #ff0000

    (Ex2) linear-gradient(yellowgreen,purple)

    (Ex3) conic-gradient(#ffa500,#00ffff)

    (Ex4) radial-gradient(yellow,green)

    (Ex5) repeating-linear-gradient(#ff00ff,transparent 5px,#4b0082 10px)

    etc...
